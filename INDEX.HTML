Cajamarca - Peru

Peru is a country that captivates with its rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and gastronomy that delights the senses. From the majestic Inca ruins of Machu Picchu to the enigmatic Nazca Lines etched in the desert, every corner of this South American country offers a unique experience for tourism enthusiasts.

The Cajamarca carnivals, they're a lively celebration that truly embodies the spirit of our city. Happening usually in February or March, it's a time when our streets burst with color, music, and dance. Everyone gets involved, from locals to visitors, in parades, dance troupes, and costume competitions.
